## üéØ Project Overview

### Primary Goal
To establish a sustainable herbal garden demonstration plot and conduct educational workshops that empower community members with knowledge about growing and utilizing medicinal and culinary herbs.

### Project Scope
The project encompasses establishing a 4-square-meter demonstration herb garden plot with ten varieties of common and beneficial herbal plants, organizing three hands-on educational workshops, creating resource materials, and documenting the entire process through activity logs and community feedback.

## üõ†Ô∏è Key Activities & Implementation

The project was structured around three core activities:

1.  **Garden Establishment:**
    * **Soil Preparation:** Clearing the 4-square-meter plot, testing soil, and amending with organic compost.
    * **Planting:** Cultivating 10 varieties of herbs using seedlings from local nurseries.
    * **Maintenance:** Regular watering, weeding, and pest management.

2.  **Educational Workshops:**
    * **Workshop 1: Basics of Herbal Plant Care:** Focused on soil, lighting, and harvesting.
    * **Workshop 2: Herbal Identification and Uses:** Covered distinguishing herbs and their traditional applications.
    * **Workshop 3: Simple Herbal Preparations:** Hands-on demos for making teas, tinctures, and oils.

3.  **Resource Creation:**
    * Developed and distributed a comprehensive **"Local Herbal Guide" pamphlet** to all participants, including identification guides, care tips, uses, and safety precautions.
